# Privacy Policy for Monochromatize

1. Data Collection
Monochromatize saves the list of domains you choose to grayscale. This data is stored locally within your browser using the Chrome Storage API.

2. Data Usage
The stored data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension: automatically applying a grayscale effect to the websites specified by the user.

3. Data Sharing
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to external servers. All settings and data remain strictly on your local device.

4. Third-Party Access
No third parties have access to your data. We do not sell or trade your information.

5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
